# Evidence Lineage Method

Evidence lineage ensures that every portfolio claim can be traced back to source material.

Internal audits use IDs such as:

```text
E-P1-API-001
E-P3-CONFLICT-004
```

Portfolio-facing cases may summarize these IDs but must not invent claims without lineage.

## Lineage rules

Every claim should be linked to at least one of:

- documented behavior
- observable behavior
- conflict summary
- branch summary
- reconstructibility finding
- human arbitration decision

## Forbidden

Do not transform an inferred behavior into a documented behavior.
