# Reconstructibility Audit Sprint

## Objective

Map where documented behavior can and cannot be reconstructed safely.

## Suitable for

- complex SaaS products
- API-first platforms
- fintech workflows
- role and permission systems
- async or event-driven behavior
- developer documentation

## Outputs

- evidence-safe findings
- branch summary
- conflict summary
- observability gaps
- reconstructibility assessment
- arbitration questions
- prioritized pressure points

## Not included

- speculative rewriting
- generic documentation best practices
- artificial workflow completion
